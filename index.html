Intro

Hello, World! I'm Param Bidja, the guy second from the left in this great team photo. I am a student at the University of Connecticut studying Computer Science & Engineering. I've worked as a teaching assistant for UConn, as well as a developer for Yale University.

Apart from work, I have many projects which I do in my spare time. Most notably, a recent project I've been involved in is rebuilding UConn's transportation mobile application. Feel free to contact me at parambidja@gmail.com if you want to chat!
